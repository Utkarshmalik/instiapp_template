Permissions and role based View

Principal View

Can view any child complete record: parent information, attendance record, circulars, examination report, etc Can view any teacher or staff data: attendance, leave, application, homework assigned, etc. Can view any classwise data: homework, classwork, timetable etc.

Parent View

Can get instant updates from school about her child fees, attendance, homework, classwork, circulars, etc. Can view child timetable, syllabus, school gallery, school calendar, teacher directory etc. Can view child examination result with performance graph.

Teacher View

Can view any child complete record as class teacher or subject teacher Can view own timetable, attendance, classwork, homework, etc. Can apply for leave, assign homework, assign examination marks, etc.

Digital Notice Board

Android Tv based Digital Notice Board

Notice board is primary thing in any school and institution and managing various notices day to day is a tedious procces so the need for such a notice board arises. This project deals with Advance notice board. Create, Manage, Update and Display the information easily on the Digital notice board. Digital notice board will help keeping all students, teachers updated with all school related activities. Digital Notice Board will help all students and teachers in getting all school related information informatioin on daily basis.
